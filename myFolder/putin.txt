Vladimir Putin (Russia)
	•	Position: President of Russia (served as President since 2000, with a break as Prime Minister from 2008–2012)
	•	Background: Former KGB officer, entered politics in the 1990s.
	•	Leadership Style: Authoritarian, centralized power, strongman image.
	•	Key Issues: Ukraine war, NATO tensions, censorship, and opposition suppression.
	•	Known For: Restoring Russian influence globally, controversial foreign policies.
